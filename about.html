Welcome to Earthy Elements

Your local retreat for all things beauty and wellness — right in the heart of Bangalore.

At Earthy Elements, we blend traditional Indian beauty rituals with modern skincare techniques. Whether it’s a soothing facial, a refreshing hair spa, or a full bridal makeover, our trained experts are here to pamper you with care and authenticity.

2015

We opened our doors in Indiranagar, Bangalore — with a mission to offer high-quality, earthy, and mindful beauty experiences.

Today

Now a trusted name across Bengaluru, we serve hundreds of happy clients every month, offering services rooted in care and community.

Tomorrow

From natural skincare to sustainable beauty, our journey continues — with plans to expand and launch our own product line.
